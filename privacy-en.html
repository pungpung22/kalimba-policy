Privacy Policy

Kalimba - Kalimba Simulator

📊 Google Play Data Safety Summary

This summary maps directly to the "Data Safety" form on Google Play Console.

  • Collected: Advertising ID, IP address, app interaction events (all automatic, via Google AdMob)
  • Shared: Advertising ID, IP, app events → Google AdMob (for advertising)
  • Not collected: name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information (offline app with no sign-up or login)
  • No data sale: we do not sell personal information.
  • Encrypted in transit: HTTPS / TLS 1.2+
  • Data deletion: uninstall the app, or clear app data in device settings.
  • Target audience: general audience (instrument simulator, not covered by the Google Play Families program)

1. Personal Information Collection

This app (Kalimba) does not collect any personal information.

Kalimba is an offline app without account registration, login, or server connectivity. Therefore, no personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted.

2. Data Stored on Device

The following data is stored only on your device when using the app:

  • App settings (background color, tine theme, volume, etc.)
  • Your recorded performances
  • Your saved sheet music

This data is not transmitted to external servers and will be deleted when the app is uninstalled.

Information we do NOT collect (data minimization)

  • Personal identifiers: name, email, phone number, date of birth, address
  • Sensitive data: national ID, credit card, bank account, biometric data, health / religious / political data
  • Communications data: SMS, call history, contacts (address book)
  • Hardware identifiers: IMEI, MAC address, serial number (only the resettable Advertising ID is used by AdMob)
  • Location: GPS or Wi-Fi-based precise location (only coarse IP-based region is inferred by AdMob)
  • On-device files: gallery scans, other apps' data, list of installed apps
  • Real-time inputs: microphone (in-app recordings are saved locally only), camera, keystrokes, screen captures

3. Third-Party Sharing

This app does not share user data with any third parties.

4. Advertising and Analytics

This app displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following for ad delivery:

  • Device identifier (Advertising ID / AAID / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • App usage events

You can limit personalized ads by resetting / deleting the Advertising ID (Android) or disabling "Allow Apps to Request to Track" (iOS).

AdMob advertising partners

AdMob may mediate or bid through Google-approved third-party ad networks (e.g., Meta Audience Network, AppLovin, Unity Ads).

  • Google-certified ad buyers and supply partners
  • How Google partner sites and apps use information
  • Google Ads Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Deletion (Google Play requirement)

This app does not store personal information on any server, so there is no "account" concept. All data resides on your device and can be fully removed via:

  • Uninstall the app: removes all local data.
  • Clear app data: Device Settings → Apps → Kalimba → Storage → Clear Data.
  • Reset Advertising ID: Device Settings → Google → Ads → Reset or delete Advertising ID to reset AdMob profile.

Note: Google Play's Account deletion requirement targets account-based apps. Since this app has no account, the requirement does not strictly apply, but an equivalent deletion path is provided above.
